Exoplanet Visualiser


How it works: Each dot on the following RA/DEC map is an exoplanet. Click on any exoplanet to get its name, mass (in Jupiter masses), distance from our solar system, and radius (in Jupiter radii). Astronomers often compare the radii and masses of exoplanets to Jupiter (e.g. A planet with 10 Jupiter masses has 10 times the mass of Jupiter) to aid mental comparison between exoplanets.
